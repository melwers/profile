BIOGRAPHY

After doing a dual course of study in Industrial Engineering and Business Management, I continued my career as a Project Manager for an engineering start-up.

Working with international clients, managing large budgets as well as leading cross-functional teams has taught me important lessons early on. Even though I liked my overarching, communicative and analytical role, I set out on becoming professional at my other passions as well.

That being said, I decided to study my master's degree in Sustainable Entrepreneurship in the Netherlands and learned how to code with Le Wagon. Besides, I am a creative and visual human being, which is why I am working as a photographer as well. While I currently work on solidifying my coding skills, my long-term vision is to create a link between software, sustainability and photography.
